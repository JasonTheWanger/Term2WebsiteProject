CrossFire CF

The first I played it is in 2012. Once my friend introduced me this fantastic First Person Shooter game, I fall in to it. It was very popular in China since 2007 and almost every single person know this superstar. In the beginning, I shot bullet while I'm moving my character, and I didn't know how to hold the recoil. I just played it because it is fun. Although I died a lot of times in the game, but I really enjoyed the time I hang out with my friend in Crossfire. Hackers had been a big issue since it was released. I really hate them but thankfully we have the voting system. This game is rolling downhill since the past 6 years due to strong and powerful "Dollar" weapon and the rule of pay to win, however, still a lot of player are playing it nowaday just like me. I wish my best to CF.
